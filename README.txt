This is the CANopen OPCUA server, the NextGen one (aka Piotr's).

What is it?
-----------

It's a smart bridge between domains of CANopen and OPCUA.
Its primary application its CERN's LHC Experiments monitoring and controls via CANopen.

Who made it?
------------

The software design is by Piotr P. Nikiel <piotr.nikiel@gmail.com>
Almost the whole implementation is also by Piotr; note the product is built on top of:
- quasar
- CanModule

Who paid for it?
----------------

CERN's ATLAS Central DCS, when Piotr was employed at CERN.
